Hackathons are communities based on learning, teaching, and creating. Thus, our focus is to build a platform to facilitate collaboration between sponsors and hackers, and enable great minds to join forces to create cool hacks. In addition, a hallmark of Hack Washington is that we accept only the brightest of all applicants, who're selected on the basis of their experience, open source contributions, demonstrated passion and aptitude for technology & innovation.
